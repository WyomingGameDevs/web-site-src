What is a Game Jam?
Build a game in 48 hours!

Global Game Jam is a 48-hour event were people come together to make games. Participants work in teams to write code, create art, compose music, construct sound effects, and design gameplay. The goal is not only to emerge with something “finished” and playable, but to also learn from one other and practice the art of game design.

FAQ

  • What is a game jam?

    A game jam is an event where people interested in game development get together to make a game quickly, usually based on a shared theme.

  • Who should attend?

    Programmers, artists, musicians, and game designers are all welcome to attend. All levels of background and experience are encouraged. Really, anyone interested in game development is welcome. Expect to learn a lot.

    Because of the time constraints, please be prepared as best you can before the event! If you haven’t made a game before, do your prep work! Try some Unity tutorials or other game design tutorials, learn your game engine/editing software, and have everything installed and setup before the event begins.

  • What is the age limit?

    Participants must be 18+ unless accompanied by an adult guardian at all times.

  • Is food provided?

    Coffee, water, and some light snacks will be provided. Please be prepared for at least 6 meals that you will provide for yourself. You can bring these with you or go out to eat at any time. There is a refrigerator and microwave on site. Please label any food you bring with you.

  • Is there room to sleep?

    There is no designated sleeping area. If you choose to sleep, please do so at an offsite location. A cat-nap at your workspace is no problem.

  • Are showers available?

    Showers are not available on site. Please shower and groom at an offsite location. Please shower before you come.

  • Where can I get more questions answered?

    You can contact us at gamejam@bisonkings.com or through the Discord Community.
